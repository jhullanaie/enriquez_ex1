Overview

History

Love and Deepspace is a 3D otome game developed by Papergames and released globally on January 18, 2024, under Infold Pte. Ltd. Known as the first of its kind in full 3D, it quickly gained popularity for its blend of romance, sci-fi, and action.

2025, the game reached 50 million players and earned over $500 million worldwide. Its success is credited to its immersive design, emotional depth, and strong appeal to female audiences, making it one of the most successful story-driven mobile games globally.
