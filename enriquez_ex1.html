All About Love and Deepspace

Overview

What is Love and deepspace?

Love and Deepspace is a cinematic 3D otome game that blends romance, sci-fi, and action into an immersive storytelling experience. Players step into the role of a heroine navigating love, memories, and danger across the stars.

With interactive 3D dates, real-time battles, and emotional narratives, you form deep connections with three unique male leads—each with their own secrets and stories. The game is praised for its lifelike visuals, voice acting, and emotionally rich choices, creating a world where your decisions truly matter.

Whether you're exploring distant galaxies or building trust and love on Earth, Love and Deepspace offers a journey that’s heartfelt, mysterious, and unforgettable.

History

Explore the origins and milestones of Love and Deepspace

Love and Deepspace is a 3D otome game developed by Papergames and released globally on January 18, 2024, under Infold Pte. Ltd. Known as the first of its kind in full 3D, it quickly gained popularity for its blend of romance, sci-fi, and action.

By 2025, the game reached 50 million players and earned over $500 million worldwide. Its success is credited to its immersive design, emotional depth, and strong appeal to female audiences, making it one of the most successful story-driven mobile games globally.
